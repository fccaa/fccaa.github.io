PhD Thesis

Dynamics of Continuous Attractor Neural Networks with Dynamical Synapses

Supervisor

Prof. K. Y. Michael Wong

Examiners

Prof. H. Benjamin Peng (Division of Life Science)
Prof. Xiangrong Wang (Department of Physics)
Prof. Bradley A. Foreman (Department of Physics)
Prof. Changsong Zhou (Hong Kong Baptist University)
Prof. Chi Keung Chan (Academia Sinica, Taiwan)

Highlights

  1. Analysis of intrinsic dynamics of continuous attractor neural networks (CANNs) with synaptic plasticity
  2. Analysis of tracking dynamics of continuous attractor with Synaptic Depression
  3. Introduction of a model that enhances perception resolution
  4. Two-dimensional CANNs with short-term synaptic plasticity
